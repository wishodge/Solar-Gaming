Resident Evil 8 Village is officially on the way and it's set to come to the PS5 in 2021. The next entry in the Resident Evil franchise is one of the highly anticipated upcoming PS5 games and takes place a few years after Resident Evil 7, featuring Ethan and Mia.

FINAL FANTASY 7

Final Fantasy 7 Remake Intergrade is the PS5 port of the JRPG released last year. Like other upcoming PS5 games, this title will take advantage of the new console's improved specs and power to deliver enhanced visuals and performance
